About Me

I am Oluwanisola Dele-Adelusi Joshua, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with a strong background in Library and Information Science. My unique combination of skills in design, development, and information management allows me to create user-centered digital solutions that bridge the gap between complex information systems and end users.

With experience in both UI/UX design and frontend development, I specialize in creating intuitive and accessible web applications. My background in Library and Information Science has given me a deep understanding of how people interact with information, which I apply to create better user experiences.

My journey in technology began with UI/UX design, where I developed a passion for creating interfaces that are both beautiful and functional. This led me to pursue frontend development, where I could bring my designs to life and have a direct impact on the user experience.
